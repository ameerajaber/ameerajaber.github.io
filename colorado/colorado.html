One of my more recent trips was to Colorado. An integral part of my everyday life is a scrumptious iced coffee, and Colorado's coffee definitely hit the spot. I tried dutch bros for the very first time, and absolutely loved it. I also had the best french toast that I still occasionally think about (if you can't tell by now I am a huge foodie!)

While in Colorado I visited beautiful botanical gardens, went ziplining, and hiked a ton! The hikes in Colorado were almost unmatched as the scenery there is so beautiful. I did get stuck on the zipline (as I usually do), but it was a ton of fun zipping over this beautiful state.
