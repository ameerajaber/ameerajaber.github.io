Growing up I've watched my fair share of Turkish shows. Loving the culture and scenery through those shows, it was only fair I visit the country myself. It is safe to say the culture and scenery is absolutely mind-boggling in reality as well. From the architecture of the country to the ferry rides to the food to the generousity of the people, Turkey is a country I will never forget.

Turkey is vast in its cities. I visited Istanbul as well as took a ferry ride from the European side to the Asian side. We also planned a trip to Cappadocia, but unfortunately it got cancelled due to weather. I do plan on returning and visiting Cappadocia and urge all travelers to look into it as well.

I recommend visiting the different mosques, especially the Hagia Sophia mosque, as well as the different palaces of rulers. I also highly recommend going to a rooftop restaurant for breakfast as the views in the morning are incredible. Turkey, overall, was a great experience and I can't wait to go back!
